About US

Established in 2015, AMY has become the go-to destination for travelers seeking seamless and secure online booking experiences. With a commitment to simplicity and reliability, we've redefined the way people travel.
At AMY, we pride ourselves on offering a comprehensive range of services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients. From domestic and international air tickets to hotel bookings, coworking space reservations, and Umrah packages, we provide it all. Our flexible payment options, including local and international debit/credit cards, bKash, Nagad, and other mobile financial services, ensure convenience for every traveler.
